## Full description (≤4000 chars)

Win dinner with picky eaters. EatPal is the meal planning and grocery app built for the messy reality of feeding a family — not for Instagram-perfect plates nobody actually eats.

**Plan meals your kids will actually eat**
Build a weekly plan in minutes. Drag recipes onto days, mix in pantry leftovers, and let EatPal flag conflicts before you commit. Tonight Mode surfaces what you can cook *right now* with what's already in the fridge.

**Track every kid's preferences**
Add a profile for each child with their allergens, "won't touch" list, and favorite foods. EatPal scores every recipe against your household — so the suggestions you see are the ones that have a real shot at the table.

**One smart grocery list, every store**
Plans roll into a tidy grocery list grouped by aisle. Mark items as you go, check what's already at home, and share the list with anyone in your household in real time.

**Family voting — end the "what's for dinner" fight**
Run a quick poll. Send three options to the household. The kids vote. You cook the winner. Done.

**Pantry that actually knows what's in it**
Snap a receipt, scan a barcode, or tap to add. EatPal tracks expiration dates and nudges you when something's about to turn so it doesn't end up in the trash.

**Health Connect ready**
Optional Health Connect integration logs nutrition from the meals you actually serve, not the ones you wished you had. Lifestyle data only — never sold, never shared, never used for ads.

**Premium**
Unlock unlimited kids, advanced filters, family voting sessions, and pantry photo intake. Free 7-day trial.

**Privacy by design**
We don't sell your data. We don't show you ads. Crash diagnostics are scrubbed of personal info. Your kid's allergen list never leaves your household.
