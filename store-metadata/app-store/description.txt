EatPal – Meal Planning for Families

Take the stress out of family mealtimes. EatPal is the all-in-one meal planning, nutrition tracking, and grocery management app built for busy parents.

KEY FEATURES

• Smart Meal Planning – Plan breakfasts, lunches, snacks, and dinners for each child with a visual weekly calendar.

• Allergen Tracking – Tag allergens per child and get warnings when meal plans include flagged ingredients.

• Nutrition Insights – Track acceptance rates, view category breakdowns, and monitor your family's nutrition progress over time.

• Barcode Scanner – Scan any packaged food to instantly add it to your pantry with full nutrition data from Open Food Facts.

• AI Meal Coach – Get personalized meal suggestions, recipe ideas, and picky-eater strategies from our AI-powered assistant.

• Food Chaining – Purpose-built tools for parents managing picky eaters, using the food chaining therapy approach to gradually expand accepted foods.

• Grocery Lists – Automatically generate grocery lists from your meal plans, check off items as you shop, and never forget an ingredient.

• Recipe Management – Save and organize family recipes with full nutritional information and easy scaling.

• Multi-Child Support – Manage separate profiles, preferences, and meal plans for every child in your family.

• Family Collaboration – Share meal plans with your partner and vote on weekly meals together.

WHO IS EATPAL FOR?

- Parents looking to simplify weekly meal prep
- Families managing food allergies or dietary restrictions
- Parents of picky eaters seeking structured feeding strategies
- Caregivers tracking nutrition for growing children
