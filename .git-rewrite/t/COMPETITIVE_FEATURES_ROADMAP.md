### **1. Smart Food Introduction System** üéØ
**Problem**: Parents don't know HOW to introduce new foods effectively
**Solution**: Guided, evidence-based food introduction protocol

**Features:**
- [ ] **Structured Exposure Ladder**
  - Start with looking at food (no touching)
  - Progress to touching, smelling, kissing
  - Advance to licking, tiny taste, small bite
  - Graduate to full portion
  - Track progress through each stage

- [ ] **Food Chaining Algorithm**
  - AI suggests similar foods based on:
    - Texture profiles
    - Flavor profiles (sweet, savory, etc.)
    - Visual similarity
    - Temperature preferences
  - Example: "Loves chicken nuggets ‚Üí Try fish sticks ‚Üí Try baked chicken"

- [ ] **Desensitization Calendar**
  - Exposure frequency recommendations
  - Optimal timing based on child's schedule
  - Parent reminders for consistent exposure

- [ ] **Success Tracking**
  - Photo timeline of attempts
  - Mood/reaction logging
  - Celebration milestones
  - Progress reports for therapists

**Why It Wins**: No competitor offers structured, therapist-approved food introduction methodology
